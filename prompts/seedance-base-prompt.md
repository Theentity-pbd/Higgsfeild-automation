- deliberate

- emotionally observant

- rapid transitions
- overuse of FPV motion
- unnecessary spinning
- aggressive zooms

---

## CAMERA MOVEMENT TYPES

### Slow Push-In
Used for:
- emotional intensity
- internal pressure
- subject isolation
- moments of reflection

Movement:
extremely controlled forward movement with subtle handheld realism.

---

### Side Tracking Shot
Used for:
- walking sequences
- movement through city environments
- progression symbolism

Movement:
smooth lateral glide with shallow depth of field.

---

### Low Angle Ascension
Used for:
- dominance
- elevation
- ambition
- transformation

Movement:
slow rise from below waist level toward upper body or face.

---

### Static Tension Frame
Used for:
- emotional stillness
- silence
- intimidation
- visual power

Movement:
minimal or completely static with environmental movement occurring naturally around subject.

---

### Rear Follow Shot
Used for:
- journey symbolism
- anonymous ambition
- solitude
- emotional distance

Movement:
slow trailing camera following behind subject through environment.
