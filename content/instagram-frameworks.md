# PBD London — Instagram Frameworks

## PURPOSE

This file defines the content frameworks, post structures, and strategic behaviour for PBD London on Instagram.

Instagram content must feel:
- premium and refined
- visually editorial
- emotionally layered
- consistent as a grid and as individual pieces
- fashion-house in presentation

---

# PLATFORM PHILOSOPHY

Instagram is a gallery, not a feed.

Every post contributes to a visual world.

PBD London on Instagram should feel like:
- browsing a luxury fashion house's campaign archive
- a curated editorial with emotional depth
- consistent, intentional, and atmospherically unified

The grid is a brand statement.
Every individual post reinforces the whole.

---

# CONTENT PILLARS

## 01 — Campaign Imagery

Primary visual output. Cinematic stills or short loops from campaign shoots.

Characteristics:
- editorial quality
- premium colour grade
- emotionally charged composition
- product visible but not the only focus

---

## 02 — Cinematic Video Content

Short-form video adapted from campaign films or standalone Reels.

Characteristics:
- slow, atmospheric, intentional
- no platform-native effects or stickers
- soundtracked with care
- 15–60 seconds

---

## 03 — Product Focus

Individual garment or product featured with editorial treatment.

Characteristics:
- premium presentation
- detail-forward
- never looks like a product listing
- context and environment add meaning

---

## 04 — Brand Philosophy

Visual or caption-led content communicating the PBD mindset.

Characteristics:
- single idea, strongly communicated
- minimal design
- emotionally resonant
- no motivational poster energy

---

## 05 — Campaign Announcements

Drop notifications, collection reveals, campaign launches.

Characteristics:
- clean and premium
- builds anticipation without urgency
- restrained language
- visual-first information delivery

---

# POST FORMATS & STRUCTURES

## Single Image Post

Primary format for campaign imagery.

Requirements:
- editorial quality composition
- cinematic colour grade
- brand-consistent visual language
- caption aligned with image atmosphere

---

## Carousel Post

Used for: multi-angle campaign storytelling, product detail series, behind-the-process content.

Structure:
- First image: strongest visual impact — this is what people see before swiping
- Middle images: expand the narrative or reveal detail
- Final image: atmospheric close or subtle CTA

Carousel logic:
- each image should work alone
- together they should tell a story
- never use carousels for information dumps

---

## Reels

Used for: campaign films, teasers, product-in-context cuts, atmospheric brand moments.

Requirements:
- vertical-adapted from widescreen master (composition must hold)
- cinematic quality maintained in vertical format
- no TikTok watermarks
- first frame is visually compelling as still

Length: 15–60 seconds preferred.

---

## Stories

Used for: behind-the-process, real-time campaign activity, limited drops, direct communication.

Characteristics:
- less polished than feed — but still intentional
- raw and real without being casual
- minimal text overlays
- never cluttered with stickers

---

# GRID STRATEGY

The grid should read as:
- a consistent visual world
- dark, cinematic, premium
- emotionally cohesive
- not perfectly alternating (avoid formulaic grid patterns)

Colour consistency:
- maintain consistent grade across posts
- primary palette dominates (black, charcoal, silver, off-white)
- accent colour moments are rare and intentional

Avoid:
- bright or warm outliers that break the atmosphere
- over-varied visual styles
- posting without considering grid impact

---

# CAPTION BEHAVIOUR ON INSTAGRAM

Captions should feel:
- premium and refined
- cinematic
- emotionally layered
- visually aligned with the image

Structure:
- short opening impact line
- emotional depth in body (2–4 lines maximum)
- restrained CTA when needed
- line breaks for pacing and rhythm

Caption length:
- short to medium
- never a wall of text
- never over-explained

Hashtags:
- 5–10 maximum
- fashion and brand relevant
- placed after the main caption, never integrated into copy

---

# ENGAGEMENT PHILOSOPHY

PBD London does not perform for engagement.

The brand creates content worth engaging with.

Preferred engagement signals:
- saves (audience finds it valuable)
- shares (audience wants to associate with it)
- profile visits (audience wants to know more)
- comments from aligned audience (fashion, ambition, discipline)

Avoid tactics:
- engagement bait captions
- question-based CTAs designed to force comments
- giveaway-driven follower acquisition

---

# POSTING RHYTHM

Quality over frequency.

Every post should feel considered.

A week of silence followed by a campaign-quality post is more powerful than daily average content.

---

# FAILURE CONDITIONS

Reject Instagram content that:
- feels rushed or underprepared
- breaks visual consistency of the grid
- looks like every other streetwear brand
- uses platform trends that dilute identity
- over-explains the brand rather than communicating it visually
